About

Built on Conviction. Guided by Principle.

McMullin Ventures is a privately held investment firm focused on opportunities where discipline, deep diligence, and a long-term orientation confer a meaningful edge.

We do not chase trends or crowd into consensus positions. Instead, we seek businesses and assets of genuine quality — enterprises with durable competitive advantages, sound management, and the capacity to compound value quietly over time.

Our approach is shaped by decades of experience across private markets, operating businesses, and strategic advisory. We bring both capital and counsel to the partnerships we choose.

I

Integrity

We hold our word above all else. Every commitment we make is one we intend to keep, fully and without reservation.

II

Patience

Exceptional outcomes are rarely quick. We resist the tyranny of the short term and measure success in years, not quarters.

III

Discernment

We say no far more often than yes. Selectivity is not timidity — it is the foundation of a sound portfolio.

What We Do

Areas of Focus

Private Investment

Direct equity and debt positions in private companies across select industries. We partner with founders and management teams who value a steady, thoughtful investor as much as they value capital.

Strategic Advisory

Counsel for business owners navigating pivotal moments — capital structure decisions, ownership transitions, and strategic repositioning. We bring perspective earned through experience, not theory.

Operating Partnership

Beyond capital, we offer active engagement with portfolio companies — assisting with governance, talent, and strategic direction where it adds genuine value and is welcome.
